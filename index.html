About This Calculator

Federal financial aid programs require you to be a U.S. citizen, U.S. national (including natives of American Samoa or Swain's Island), or permanent resident of the U.S. Other students are not eligible for US federal financial aid programmes and should not use this tool.

You are NOT applying for financial aid when you complete this Calculator. To apply for financial aid, you should file the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and follow the instructions found on https://www.ucd.ie/global/study-at-ucd/scholarshipsfinances/northamericaloans/usfederalaid/

Financial aid estimates are based on full-time undergraduate enrollment and do not include merit-based scholarships or private scholarships from outside donors. Also, students using federal and/or state military benefits may have aid adjustments not captured in this estimate.

The information you provide and the estimated results you receive will not be stored by University College Dublin. You should print the results if you wish to retain a record.

⚠️ Please note: The estimate generated by the calculator is for informational purposes only. It does not represent a final determination, or actual offer, of financial assistance. It is not binding and may not reflect all recent updates to US federal loan programmes.
